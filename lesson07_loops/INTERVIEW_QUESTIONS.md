# Lesson 07: Interview Questions

## Beginner Questions
* **Q**: What are the types of loops in Kotlin?
  **A**: `for`, `while`, and `do-while`.

## Intermediate Questions
* **Q**: How do you loop backwards from 10 to 1 in Kotlin?
  **A**: Using the `downTo` keyword, like: `for (i in 10 downTo 1)`.
